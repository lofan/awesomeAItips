The video **"The 2026 LinkedIn Algorithm EXPLAINED"** by Josefine Wanner provides a strategic guide for coaches and entrepreneurs to navigate recent shifts in LinkedIn's algorithm. The core message is to move away from "polished, corporate" content toward a "human-first" approach that prioritizes genuine connection over viral reach.

### **Summary of Key Points**

#### **1. The Move Toward Human-First Content**
* **Ditch the Corporate Speak:** LinkedIn's algorithm now favors genuine, human language over "stiff" or "polished" corporate jargon [[02:04](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=67nUV7byFy8&t=124)]. 
* **Vulnerability with a Purpose:** Sharing mistakes and struggles builds trust, but Wanner advises sharing them only **after** you have overcome them. Sharing while in the "middle of the mess" may garner pity rather than professional respect [[02:44](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=67nUV7byFy8&t=164)].
* **Conversations vs. Broadcasts:** The platform prioritizes posts that spark dialogue rather than those that just shout information at an audience [[02:12](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=67nUV7byFy8&t=132)].

#### **2. Strategic Content Placement**
* **Topic Authority:** You must "claim a topic" so the algorithm knows where to "box" you. This helps LinkedIn show your content to people interested in that niche, even if they aren't your first-degree connections [[03:13](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=67nUV7byFy8&t=193)].
* **Use Your Client’s Language:** Avoid "coaching speak." If you talk like a coach, you will attract other coaches. To attract clients, use the specific language and pain points your ideal clients use [[03:51](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=67nUV7byFy8&t=231)].

#### **3. Quality and Consistency Over Quantity**
* **The "Sweet Spot":** You don't need to post every day. Posting **2 to 3 times per week** is considered ideal for maintaining quality and relevance [[05:23](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=67nUV7byFy8&t=323)].
* **Engagement is Key:** The algorithm tracks your activity. You must engage with your own comments and other people's posts to signal that you are building a community, not just using a "megaphone" [[05:34](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=67nUV7byFy8&t=334)].
* **Value New Metrics:** High-value creators are identified by "dwell time" (how long someone stays on your post), saves, and shares, rather than just raw like counts [[06:09](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=67nUV7byFy8&t=369)].

#### **4. Overcoming Growth Hurdles**
* **Imposter Syndrome:** Many creators post "safe" content because they fear judgment from former colleagues. Wanner encourages creators to ignore these fears, as raw and real content is what actually performs [[01:42](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=67nUV7byFy8&t=102)].
* **Strategic Call to Action (CTA):** Every post should have **one** clear "call to engagement." Don't try to sell in every post, but be intentional about what you want the reader to do next [[06:48](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=67nUV7byFy8&t=408)].
