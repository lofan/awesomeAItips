### [Top 10 Must-Read Books on Artificial Intelligence (AI) in 2026](https://www.index.dev/blog/best-ai-books-engineering-leaders#the-ai-driven-leader-harnessing-ai-to-make-faster-smarter-decisions-by-geoff-woods)

AI Engineering & Technical Implementation
These books focus on the "how-to" of building, designing, and deploying models.
* [AI Engineering: Building Applications with Foundation Models by Chip Huyen](https://www.amazon.com/AI-Engineering-Building-Applications-Foundation/dp/1098166302)
* [Designing Machine Learning Systems by Chip Huyen](https://www.amazon.com/Designing-Machine-Learning-Systems-Production-Ready/dp/1098107969)
* [Hands-On Large Language Models: Language Understanding and Generation by Jay Alammar and Maarten Grootendorst](https://www.amazon.com/Hands-Large-Language-Models-Understanding/dp/1098150961)
* [Building Agentic AI Systems: Create Intelligent, Autonomous AI Agents by Anjanava Biswas and Wrick Talukdar](https://www.amazon.com/Building-Agentic-Systems-intelligent-autonomous/dp/1803238755)
* [The LLM Engineering Handbook by Paul Iusztin and Maxime Labonne](https://www.amazon.com/LLM-Engineers-Handbook-engineering-production/dp/1836200072)
* [Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach (4th Edition) by Stuart Russell and Peter Norvig](https://www.amazon.com/Artificial-Intelligence-A-Modern-Approach/dp/0134610997)

Strategy, Leadership & Society
These books are geared toward navigating the impact of AI on business and the global landscape.
* [Co-Intelligence: Living and Working with AI by Ethan Mollick](https://www.amazon.com/Co-Intelligence-Living-Working-Ethan-Mollick/dp/059371671X)
* [The AI-Driven Leader: Harnessing AI to Make Faster, Smarter Decisions by Geoff Woods](https://www.amazon.com/AI-Driven-Leader-Harnessing-Smarter-Decisions/dp/B0DB8QL3ZK)
* [The AI-Savvy Leader: Nine Ways to Take Back Control and Make AI Work by David De Cremer](https://www.amazon.com/AI-Savvy-Leader-Nine-Ways-Control/dp/1647826233)
* [Supremacy: AI, ChatGPT, and the Race That Will Change the World by Parmy Olson](https://www.amazon.com/Supremacy-ChatGPT-Race-Change-World/dp/1035038226)
